The Spicy BBQ Chicken Pizza

This mouthwatering pizza features a crispy, pre-baked crust smothered in spicy barbecue sauce and topped with juicy chicken breast, tangy pepperoncini peppers, sweet red onions, fresh cilantro, and melty Colby-Jack cheese. Perfect for pizza night, this recipe is easy to make and full of flavor.


Ingredients:

  • 1 (12 inch) pre-baked pizza crust
  • 1 cup spicy barbeque sauce
  • 2 skinless boneless chicken breast halves, cooked and cubed
  • 1 cup sliced pepperoncini peppers
  • 1 cup chopped red onion
  • Â½ cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 cups shredded Colby-Jack cheese

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Place pizza crust on a baking sheet. Spread barbeque sauce on crust. Top with chicken, pepperoncini peppers, onion, and cilantro. Cover with Colby-Jack cheese.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted and bubbly, about 15 minutes.
